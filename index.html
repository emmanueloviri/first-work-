DEVELOPMENT MICROFINANCE BANK Wetland Microfinance bank started as Erikpo Community Bank in year 2000 and in 2005 following the transformation of community banks to Microfinance Bank and the subsequent policy on Microfinance, thus brought the present name (Wetland Microfinance Bank). The Bank has being a catalyst for economic development in rural areas and among the urban poor; especially the low income people and poor in the Niger-Delta community since she acquired her CBN state license. The Bank is achieving her drive which entails the use of a systematic approach that ensure individual’s re-orientation and empowerment of the individual rural dwellers for grass root financial inclusion and development in last seventeen years
